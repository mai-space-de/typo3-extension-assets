===============
Maispace Assets
===============

:Extension key:
    maispace_assets

:Package name:
    maispace/assets

:Version:
    1.0.0

:Language:
    en

:Description:
    Easy inline or path-based asset inclusion (CSS, JS, SCSS) from Fluid templates.
    Performance-focused: minification, deferred loading, caching, and SVG sprite building.
